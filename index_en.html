Features

These are some of the characteristics of the AIKE farm. For more information, please contact us.

Excellent location

Access by dirt road, in good condition, just 5 kilometers from Route 8. The farm is located 80 kilometers from Montevideo and 50 kilometers from Punta del Este.

Wind and solar energy

Wind turbine of 2KW in 48V and a table of solar panels of 3KW. The entire power system is controlled from a booth built especially for this purpose.

Installations

Main house of solid traditional construction, main shed with games room, house for the caretaker with 2 bedrooms and stables with facilities for the staff.

Facilities

50 m2 house with double brick walls, living room with stove, bedroom, bathroom, closed barbecue with integrated kitchen, barbecue area and clay oven. It also has a covered garage with electric gate.

House for landlord

House of 30 square meters with 2 floors. Kitchen, living-dining room and 2 bedrooms. Patio with eaves of French tiles with barbecue and bathroom.

Main shed

Of 140 square meters, with eaves on its front of French tiles. It has a bathroom, 6 windows, a mezzanine of 40 square meters and a games room.
